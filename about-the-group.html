Who We Are

An amateur drama group based in North Kerry, bringing new drama to the stage.

BOG ROAD Theatre is a grassroots ensemble deeply rooted in the soil of Kerry. We believe that the most powerful stories are those grown from our own communities. Our ethos is built on the amateur spirit, the love of the craft for its own sake. We have a fierce commitment to staging original, contemporary drama that speaks to the modern Irish experience while honoring our traditional heritage.
